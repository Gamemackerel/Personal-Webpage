Hi, I'm Abe


I live in Seattle, WA and work as a software engineer specializing in biotech software. I studied computer science and philosophy at UW, and I currently work on laboratory automation and orchestration solutions for Highres Bio.

I believe that technology, when it is developed with caution and intention, can make powerful positive impacts on society. My hope is to build such technology, in collaboration with likeminded folks.

In my leisure time I enjoy movies, reading, playing games, bicycling, rollerskating, drinking coffee, patting dogs or cats, and discussing politics. I also write occasionally on my blog.
