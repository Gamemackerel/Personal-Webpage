SettleChip App Privacy Policy

Introduction

This policy regards SettleChip: Poker Calculator (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is secure. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data, emphasizing that the App stores your data solely on your local device and does not make any network connections. Therefore, your data is not shared with anyone.

Data Collection

Personal Data

The App does not collect any personal data. Since all data is stored locally on your device, we do not have access to, nor do we store, any information that could identify you personally.

Usage Data

The App does not collect any usage data. There are no analytics, tracking, or monitoring mechanisms within the App. All interactions and data remain strictly within your device. Data Storage

Local Storage

All data created and managed within the App is stored locally on your device. This means that your data is never transmitted over the internet or shared with external servers.

Data Sharing

No Sharing

The App does not share any data with third parties. Since there are no network connections, there is no possibility of data being transmitted to any external entities.

User Rights

Control Over Your Data

As the data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can modify, delete, or transfer your data as you see fit.

Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your data, deleting the App from your device will remove all data associated with it.

Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the App's update notes and within the App itself. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data. However, we plan to maintain a local, simple, and fully private app.
